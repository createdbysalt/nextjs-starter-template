# Page-Type Benchmarks

## How to Use This Reference

These benchmarks define what elements are expected on each page type. Use during audits to quickly identify missing elements. An element marked "Required" should flag as HIGH severity if missing. "Recommended" elements flag as MEDIUM if missing.

---

## Homepage

### Purpose
Orient visitors, build initial trust, route to appropriate paths.

### Required Elements

| Element | Why Required | Audit Check |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Clear value proposition | Visitor must know what you do | □ Present above fold |
| Primary CTA | Must be clear action to take | □ Visible without scroll |
| Navigation | Access to key pages | □ Logical, ≤7 items |
| Trust signal | Initial credibility | □ At least 1 above fold |

### Recommended Elements

| Element | Why Recommended | Audit Check |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Secondary CTA | For not-ready visitors | □ Email capture or softer action |
| Social proof section | Build confidence | □ Logos, testimonials, or stats |
| How it works | Reduce confusion | □ 3-step or visual explanation |
| Feature/benefit highlights | Show value | □ Scannable format |
| Segmented paths | For multiple audiences | □ Clear routing options |

### Red Flags to Catch

- [ ] No clear indication of what you sell/do
- [ ] CTA below the fold
- [ ] Multiple competing CTAs with equal weight
- [ ] No trust signals visible
- [ ] Stock image with no relevance
- [ ] "Welcome to our website" type headline

### Benchmark Questions

1. Can a stranger identify what you do in 5 seconds?
2. Is there one clear primary action?
3. Would someone trust this enough to continue?

## About Page

### Purpose
Build trust, humanize the brand, tell the story.

### Required Elements

| Element | Why Required | Audit Check |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Company story/mission | Humanization and connection | □ Authentic, not corporate-speak |
| Team or founder | People trust people | □ Photos + real names |
| Contact information | Trust signal | □ Email, phone, or address |

### Recommended Elements

| Element | Why Recommended | Audit Check |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Values or beliefs | Connection with aligned customers | □ Specific, not generic |
| Credentials/awards | Authority building | □ Relevant achievements |
| Media mentions | Third-party validation | □ Logos or quotes |
| Company history | Establishes longevity | □ Timeline or narrative |
| CTA | Don't waste the visit | □ Appropriate next step |

### Red Flags to Catch

- [ ] No people shown (just stock images)
- [ ] Generic mission statement
- [ ] No contact information
- [ ] Dead-end page (no CTA)
- [ ] Corporate jargon heavy
- [ ] Claims without proof

### Benchmark Questions

1. Would you trust this company after reading this?
2. Do you know who you're doing business with?
3. Is there a clear next step?

## Product/Service Page

### Purpose
Explain offering, overcome objections, drive conversion.

### Required Elements

| Element | Why Required | Audit Check |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Clear product name/title | Identification | □ H1 present and clear |
| Benefits | "What's in it for me" | □ Not just features |
| Pricing or path to pricing | Decision enablement | □ Visible or clear next step |
| Primary CTA | Conversion path | □ "Buy", "Book", "Start" |
| Key features | Understanding what they get | □ Specific to this offering |

### Recommended Elements

| Element | Why Recommended | Audit Check |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Testimonials | Social proof | □ Relevant to this product |
| FAQ | Objection handling | □ Addresses common questions |
| Comparison (vs alternatives) | For comparison shoppers | □ Honest, favorable framing |
| Risk reducer | Lower barrier | □ Guarantee, trial, refund |
| Related products | Cross-sell / alternatives | □ Don't distract from primary |
| Feature-benefit connection | Value clarity | □ "Feature X so you can Y" |

### Red Flags to Catch

- [ ] Features without benefits
- [ ] No pricing or path to pricing
- [ ] Missing CTA
- [ ] No social proof specific to product
- [ ] Objections unaddressed
- [ ] Generic stock imagery
- [ ] "Contact us for pricing" without context

### Benchmark Questions

1. Do I understand what I'm getting?
2. Do I believe it works?
3. Can I easily take the next step?

## Pricing Page

### Purpose
Enable purchase decision, justify value, reduce friction.

### Required Elements

| Element | Why Required | Audit Check |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Clear pricing tiers | Decision enablement | □ Easy to understand |
| What's included | Value understanding | □ Per tier clarity |
| CTA per tier | Conversion path | □ Obvious action per option |
| Currency/billing cycle | Clarity | □ No hidden surprises |

### Recommended Elements

| Element | Why Recommended | Audit Check |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Recommended tier | Decision simplification | □ "Most popular" or similar |
| Feature comparison table | Easy comparison | □ Visual, scannable |
| FAQ | Objection handling | □ Pricing-specific questions |
| Guarantee | Risk reduction | □ Money-back or satisfaction |
| Social proof | Value validation | □ ROI testimonial or stats |
| Annual vs monthly | Upsell opportunity | □ Clear savings shown |

### Red Flags to Catch

- [ ] Too many tiers (confusion)
- [ ] No clear recommendation
- [ ] Hidden fees or surprises
- [ ] Comparison table too complex
- [ ] No guarantee or risk reducer
- [ ] Pricing only via contact
- [ ] Missing "per seat" or unit clarity

### Benchmark Questions

1. Can I quickly identify the right option for me?
2. Do I understand exactly what I'm paying?
3. Is there a reason not to buy today?

## Contact Page

### Purpose
Make contact easy, capture leads, provide reassurance.

### Required Elements

| Element | Why Required | Audit Check |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Contact form | Primary capture | □ Working, minimal fields |
| Email address | Alternative contact | □ Clickable mailto: |
| Response time expectation | Reduce anxiety | □ "We respond within X" |

### Recommended Elements

| Element | Why Recommended | Audit Check |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Phone number | Immediacy option | □ With hours if applicable |
| Physical address | Trust signal | □ For applicable businesses |
| Map | Local businesses | □ Embedded or linked |
| FAQ | Pre-answer common questions | □ Reduces form submissions |
| Social links | Alternative channels | □ Where you're actually active |
| Success confirmation | Completion clarity | □ What happens next |

### Red Flags to Catch

- [ ] Too many form fields
- [ ] No confirmation after submit
- [ ] No response time indication
- [ ] Contact info buried
- [ ] Captcha on simple form
- [ ] Required phone number
- [ ] No alternative to form

### Benchmark Questions

1. Can someone reach you in under 30 seconds?
2. Do they know what happens after submission?
3. Is there an option for urgent needs?

## Landing Page (Campaign/Ads)

### Purpose
Convert traffic from specific source with single focus.

### Required Elements

| Element | Why Required | Audit Check |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Headline matching ad/source | Message match | □ Continues the promise |
| Single, clear CTA | Focused conversion | □ One action |
| Value proposition | Why act | □ Above fold |
| Social proof | Credibility | □ Near CTA |
| Form or CTA button | Conversion mechanism | □ Minimal friction |

### Recommended Elements

| Element | Why Recommended | Audit Check |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Hero image/video | Visual engagement | □ Relevant, not stock |
| Benefits (3-5) | Value support | □ Scannable |
| Testimonial | Trust | □ Relevant to offer |
| Urgency (if real) | Action driver | □ Authentic |
| Trust badges | Credibility | □ Near form |
| Privacy assurance | Friction reducer | □ Near form |

### Red Flags to Catch

- [ ] Multiple competing CTAs
- [ ] Navigation present (distraction)
- [ ] Message mismatch with ad
- [ ] Too much content
- [ ] Form too long for offer value
- [ ] No trust signals
- [ ] Generic headline

### Benchmark Questions

1. Would someone from [ad source] feel this is what they clicked for?
2. Is there only ONE thing to do?
3. Is the value worth the ask?

## Blog Post / Content Page

### Purpose
Attract organic traffic, build authority, nurture toward conversion.

### Required Elements

| Element | Why Required | Audit Check |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Clear, relevant headline | Search + engagement | □ Answers searcher intent |
| Quality content | Value delivery | □ Substantive, not thin |
| Author attribution | Trust | □ Name, ideally with bio |
| Readable formatting | Consumption | □ Headers, short paragraphs |

### Recommended Elements

| Element | Why Recommended | Audit Check |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Related content | Engagement | □ Internal links |
| CTA (contextual) | Conversion path | □ Relevant to topic |
| Email capture | Nurture | □ For not-ready visitors |
| Social sharing | Amplification | □ Easy to share |
| Publication date | Freshness | □ Shows relevance |
| Table of contents | Long content | □ For posts >1500 words |

### Red Flags to Catch

- [ ] No path to conversion
- [ ] No related content
- [ ] Wall of text formatting
- [ ] No author shown
- [ ] Outdated information
- [ ] Dead end (no next step)
- [ ] Keyword stuffing

### Benchmark Questions

1. Does this answer what the searcher was looking for?
2. Is there a logical next step?
3. Would this build trust in the brand?

## Checkout / Signup Flow

### Purpose
Complete the conversion with minimum friction.

### Required Elements

| Element | Why Required | Audit Check |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Order/signup summary | Clarity | □ What they're getting |
| Total cost (all-in) | No surprises | □ Including fees, tax |
| Security indicators | Trust | □ SSL, badges |
| Clear submit action | Completion | □ Specific button copy |
| Error handling | Recovery | □ Clear, helpful errors |

### Recommended Elements

| Element | Why Recommended | Audit Check |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Progress indicator | Multi-step clarity | □ Shows where they are |
| Trust reinforcement | Final confidence | □ Testimonial or guarantee |
| Guest checkout option | Friction reduction | □ No forced registration |
| Multiple payment options | Flexibility | □ Cards, PayPal, etc. |
| Support access | Safety net | □ Chat or help link |
| Cart abandonment capture | Recovery | □ Email for recovery |

### Red Flags to Catch

- [ ] Surprise fees at end
- [ ] Forced account creation
- [ ] Too many steps
- [ ] No security indicators
- [ ] Unclear total cost
- [ ] No guest option
- [ ] Confusing coupon entry

### Benchmark Questions

1. Is it clear exactly what they're paying?
2. Is the process as short as possible?
3. Do they feel safe entering payment info?

## Case Study / Success Story

### Purpose
Prove results with specific evidence.

### Required Elements

| Element | Why Required | Audit Check |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Customer identification | Credibility | □ Real company/person |
| Challenge/problem | Relatability | □ What they faced |
| Solution | Your role | □ How you helped |
| Results | Proof | □ Specific, quantified |

### Recommended Elements

| Element | Why Recommended | Audit Check |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Customer quote | Voice authenticity | □ In their words |
| Metrics/data | Concrete proof | □ Numbers, percentages |
| Visuals | Engagement | □ Screenshots, before/after |
| Industry/use case tag | Relevance filtering | □ For similar prospects |
| CTA | Conversion path | □ "Get similar results" |
| Related case studies | Cross-sell | □ Similar audience/problem |

### Red Flags to Catch

- [ ] No specific results
- [ ] Anonymous customer
- [ ] Generic outcomes
- [ ] No visuals
- [ ] Dead end (no CTA)
- [ ] Challenge doesn't match ICP

### Benchmark Questions

1. Is this proof believable and verifiable?
2. Would the target ICP see themselves in this?
3. Is there a clear path to get similar results?

## FAQ Page

### Purpose
Answer common questions, handle objections at scale.

### Required Elements

| Element | Why Required | Audit Check |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Categorized questions | Findability | □ Logical grouping |
| Clear answers | Value | □ Actually helpful |
| Search function | For large FAQs | □ If >15 questions |

### Recommended Elements

| Element | Why Recommended | Audit Check |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Objection-focused Qs | Conversion support | □ Not just logistics |
| Links to detail pages | Depth | □ Learn more options |
| Contact fallback | When FAQ fails | □ "Still have questions?" |
| CTA | Conversion path | □ Relevant to FAQ topic |
| Schema markup | SEO | □ FAQ rich snippets |

### Red Flags to Catch

- [ ] Questions no one asks
- [ ] Missing major objections
- [ ] No way to get more help
- [ ] Wall of text answers
- [ ] Dead end page
- [ ] Outdated information

### Benchmark Questions

1. Are the real objections addressed?
2. Can someone easily find what they need?
3. Is there a next step when answered?
